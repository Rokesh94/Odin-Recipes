Fish Balls

Great for when you don't have enough fish to feed the family.

  • 1 pound white fish fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 ½ cups mashed potatoes
  • ½ cup chopped green onions
  1. Season fish with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook fish in the hot skillet until almost completely cooked through, turning as needed, 7 to 10 minutes. Remove from heat.
  2. Transfer fish to a bowl and flake with a fork. Add mashed potatoes, green onions, egg, flour, and Creole seasoning. Mix thoroughly. Add panko, a tablespoon at a time, as needed to firm up balls. Form mixture into small balls or patties.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
